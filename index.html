Home

Welcome to my personal website. It's great to have you view works and show your interest in collaborating.
I am a Christian, a minister of the gospel, a leadership coach, and a Statistician.
My research work is focused on developing statistical methods to account for biases in biodiversity data. My modelling competencies span a wide range of ecological models: Species Distribution Model, Spatio-temporal modelling, Integrated Distribution/Population modelling, etc.
I am also particularly interested in how Statistics can be applied to theological and daily church affairs.
